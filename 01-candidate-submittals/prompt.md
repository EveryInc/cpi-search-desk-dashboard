# Candidate Submittal Skill — Demo Prompts

Two synthetic resumes in this folder (Priya Nadkarni, Owen Brackett — both also appear in the
dashboard data in `00-search-desk-dashboard/`). Use them to build your first skill.

**Step 1 — first draft (paste into Claude):**

> Read priya-nadkarni-resume.pdf and write a candidate submittal blurb I could send a client.

**Step 2 — make it yours (edit to taste, this is the point):**

> Rewrite it: lead with years in role and deal experience, name the strategy fit,
> flag timeline/availability, keep it under 120 words, no adjectives like "rockstar."

**Step 3 — lock it in:**

> Save this as a skill called candidate-submittal so every submittal blurb comes out
> in exactly this format.

**Step 4 — prove it (new chat):**

> Use the candidate-submittal skill on owen-brackett-resume.pdf.

Same format, different candidate. That's the whole lesson: Claude edits your files,
and skills make it consistent.
